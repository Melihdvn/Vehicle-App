 | Issue: Engine won't start.

 | Issue: Strange engine noises.

 | Issue: Car pulls left/right.
 | Answer: Align wheels, check tire pressure.

 | Issue: Brake pedal spongy.
 | Answer: Bleed brake system, check fluid.

 | Issue: AC not cooling.
 | Answer: Check refrigerant levels, compressor.

 | Issue: Transmission slipping.
 | Answer: Check fluid level, inspect transmission.
